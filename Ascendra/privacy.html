Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 3, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Ascendra collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Ascendra stores information that you choose to enter, including:

  • Calendar events
  • Journal entries
  • To-do lists
  • Habits
  • App settings

How Your Information Is Stored

Currently, all information is stored locally on your device using your web browser's Local Storage. Your information is not sent to our servers.

Data Sharing

Ascendra does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Cookies

Ascendra does not currently use cookies for tracking or advertising.

Security

We work to keep your information safe. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Control

You may delete your stored information at any time by clearing your browser data or using features within Ascendra that remove saved information.

Future Updates

If Ascendra adds cloud sync, user accounts, or additional online features, this Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect those changes.

If you want to be more secure, use two step verification to keep data safe from anyone but you!
